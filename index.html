What Makes Zen Garden Interesting

My first impression of the Zen Garden is about its neat organization, which makes me feel really comfortable in browsing the website. The comfort comes from the success of many areas: the layout, the collocation of the color (green can make people even more comfortable), the background, and the font. The designer not only works on the overall outlook, but also pays a lot of attention to those minature details. For instance, at the very first when the audience try to look at the links to download both HTML and CSS files, there would be a darker green hover. It is of course not hard to design a hover, but the color matches really well. Those details make the whole website better. And the Zen Garden provides many good designs we can use for reference. They are all really neat in terms of the the design of the page.


My Favorite CSS Design off of the Website

The Address
